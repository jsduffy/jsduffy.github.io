Design Principles

Together with the Ministry of Education, the ECASE project has worked to clearly identify guiding design principles for the establishment of new STEM schools in Egypt.
  1. Value and build all school structures, including project-based learning, to prepare students to demonstrate their ability to think independently, creatively and analytically
  2. Prepare students to be scientifically, mathematically and technologically literate and proficient, able to apply their understanding to advance creativity, innovation and invention with a real-world vision
  3. Promote an environment of questioning, collaboration and high level of communication
  4. Value and promote self-motivation, self–direction and lifelong learning
  5. Engage partnerships to accelerate school capacity and broaden student, teacher and administrative opportunity
  6. Ensure all graduating students will be admitted to and competitive in a university and then in the Egyptian labor market as well as the world labor market.
  7. Foster the development of socially responsible leaders with a consciousness and eye toward amore contemporary Egypt – solving the future while addressing the largest grand challenges

Grand Challenges

As part of the intial curriculum design studio, experts from across Egypt identified a set of ten grand challenges: pressing issues which Egypt must resolve in the future. These challenges form the core of the curriculum, providing context and meaning for learning.
  • Address the exponential population growth and prepare for the impact
  • Improve the use of alternative energies to reduce our reliance on extracted fuel sources
  • Reduce urban congestion and its impact
  • Increase opportunities for Egyptians to stay and work in Egypt
  • Work to eradicate public health issues/disease
  • Increase efficient use of our land through improved use of arid areas
  • Improve Sources of Clean Water
  • Increase industrial base for Egypt
  • Reduce pollution fouling our air water and grounds
  • Recycle and retain garbage for recycling
